Scientific name: Ophiophagus hannah

All About Them:

  1. Habitat

    2. King cobras are mainly found in Asia, especially India and China. They live near streams around many dense, tree-filled areas such as (rain)forests and swamps because they are cold-blooded and the environmental conditions do not change quite often.

  2. Scales
  3. Color
  4. Prey

    5. King cobras are carnivorous creatures. They eat various things such as small mammals like rodents, reptiles like lizards, birds, and even other snakes like pythons. Since they have such a slow metabolism, they eat only one meal every couple months.
