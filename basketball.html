Sport created by James Naismith in 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts

Why You Should Play

Basketball is fun! You can play with 1-9 other people, at it's super simple. It doesn't even matter how skilled you are: you don't have to play a game since you can just go to a court and shoot around too.

How to Play

Basketball is played on a court with two teams of five players on each side. The objective is to score as many points as possible in four twelve-minute quarters by putting the ball inside the ten-foot-tall hoop via a field goal (which can be worth two points of three points depending on how far the ball is shot from the basket) or a free throw (worth one point that is given to a player from fouls). There are many types of ways to get the ball into the basket, such as a jumpshot, layup, hook shot, dunk, et cetera.

Where to Play

Basketball courts can be found in many places, both indoors and outdoors. They can be found in public parks as well as private gyms. Many people love to play, and it does not have to be the traditional 5v5 either. 1v1s, 2v2s, 3v3s, and 4v4s can also be played, except they are normally played with only half of the court. Basically, as long as there is somewhere with a hoop and a ball, then this sport can be played.

Some First Steps

The best place to start is to just get a basketball, go to a court, and play. It'd be more fun to invite other people to play with you too, but you can play with anyone at the court and build new bonds. Also, it would be advised to buy basketball shoes.
