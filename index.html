The Big Problem

Snakes are known to be one of the most deadly and vicious animals in the wild. This is mostly due to their loud hissing, their preying on other animals considered cute (bunnies, gerbils, hamsters, etc.), and the venom in their ferocious bite. But what most people don't know is that snakes are very endangered due to certain factors, many attibuted to human activities.
