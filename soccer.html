Sport created in the Han Dynasty

Why You Should Play

Soccer is probably the most popular sport in the world. It is such a fun sport to play, and when there are giant games, everyone gathers as a competitive community to watch it. It honestly requires the most overall fitness compared to any other sport, as strength, speed, and endurance are critical. It is also a great way to meet new people.

How to Play

There are two goals at each side of the field. Each team is allowed 11 players on the field. No position like the forward, midfielder, or defender are allowed to use their hands except for the goalkeeper; they're only allowed to use their legs, head, etc. The main objective is to score the soccer ball past the goal line as many times as possible, and whoever does so at the end of ninety minutes is the winner.

Where to Play

You can play at a turf field, which can have their own goals or you could use anything as a post. Pretty much any area with grass is a feasible place to play soccer. You can also play indoors as well.

Some First Steps

Grab a soccer ball and go play! You can practice with your friends by just passing the ball to each other or using cones and doing drills and trying tricks. It's really fun to shoot against each other too. You could also get turf shoes or even cleats for playing.
