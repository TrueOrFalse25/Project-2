Badminton 🏸

Why You Should Play

Badminton is a fun-total body sport! It's fun to play with friends, and it's extremely portable because all you really need is a racket and a shuttlecock. You can meet a lot of great people playing this sport too.

How to Play

Badminton requires two to four players to play. Players have only one hit to hit the shuttlecock over the net. It goes back and forth and whoever's side the shuttlecock hits the ground, the opposing team gains a point. Badminton is played to 21 points, where the winning team is winning by 2; if not, first to 30 wins.

Where to Play

Pretty much anywhere! Just find a spot with adequate room to move back and forth and a good amount of space in the air for the shuttlecock to be hit. This could be in the park, on the sidewalk, etc.

Some First Steps

Just have fun and play! Badminton can be one of the most casual sports, and it's just fun to watch that shuttlecock fly back and forth and people dive to prevent it from touching the ground.
